Deck has 52 cards

Players:
- computer dealer
- human player

Begining:
- human player places a bet
- dealer starts with 1 card face up and 1 card face down
- human starts with 2 cards face up
- human player goes first
- after goes the computer dealer until the games end

Goal:
- human player has to get closer to a total value of 21 than the dealer does

Possible actions (human player):
- Hit: receive a card from the deck
- Stay: stop receiving cards

Possible actions (computer dealer):
- If the player is under 21, dealer must hit until it gets more than the player and right
or under 21, or until its loses when gets above 21 (gets busted)

End game:
- human player stay and the computer dealer gets more than him and right or under 21
- human player or computer dealer gets above 21 (gets busted)

Special rules:
- Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) count as 10
- Aces can count as 1 or 11, the player must decide
